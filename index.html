ASIAN

Breakfast
1/4
chicken curry

2 tablespoons olive oil;

2 large onions diced;

1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste;

2 large tomatoes diced;

1 teaspoon cayenne pepper;

or more to taste

1 teaspoon curry powder

1 teaspoon garam masala

1 teaspoon ground turmeric

1 teaspoon ground cumin

4 skinless boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces

2 large red-skinned potatoes;

chopped1/2 cup fresh cilantro;

Add all ingredients to list

Directions

Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir the onions in the hot oil until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the ginger-garlic paste and continue cooking another 5 minutes. Reduce heat to medium; stir the tomatoes into the mixture and cook until the tomatoes are pulpy, 5 to 10 minutes. Season with the cayenne pepper, curry powder, garam masala, turmeric, and cumin; cook and stir another 5 minutes. Add the chicken and potatoes to the mixture in the skillet; simmer, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are tender and the chicken is no longer pink in the center, about 20 minutes. Sprinkle the cilantro over the mixture and continue simmering another 10 minutes. Serve hot.

braised balsamic chicken

6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

1 teaspoon garlic

saltground black pepper to taste

2 tablespoons olive oil1 onion, thinly sliced

1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes

1/2 cup balsamic vinegar

1 teaspoon dried basil

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1 teaspoon dried rosemary

1/2 teaspoon dried thyme

Directions

Season both sides of chicken breasts with garlic salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook seasoned chicken breasts until chicken is browned, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Add onion; cook and stir until onion is browned, 3 to 4 minutes. Pour diced tomatoes and balsamic vinegar over chicken; season with basil, oregano, rosemary and thyme. Simmer until chicken is no longer pink and the juices run clear, about 15 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).

Directions

Dust the veal shanks lightly with flour. Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium to medium-high heat. Add the veal, and cook until browned on the outside. Remove to a bowl, and keep warm. Add two cloves of crushed garlic and onion to the skillet; cook and stir until onion is tender. Return the veal to the pan and mix in the carrot and wine. Simmer for 10 minutes. Pour in the tomatoes and beef stock, and season with salt and pepper. Cover, and simmer over low heat for 1 1/2 hours, basting the veal every 15 minutes or so. The meat should be tender, but not falling off the bone. In a small bowl, mix together the parsley, 1 clove of garlic and lemon zest. Sprinkle the gremolata over the veal just before serving.

Chicken Biryani The Ingridients
  1. 1.5 kg Chicken (remove skin)
  2. 4 cans of crashed tomatoes (8 to 10 tomatoes chopped and blend)
  3. 2 small cans of tomato paste
  4. 4 large onions
  5. Teaspoon of ginger and garlic paste
  6. 1 tablespoon of cooking oil or ghee butter
  7. 1/2 glass of plain yoghurt
  8. 6 eggs
  9. Mint,corriander,mixed spice,garam masala spice,curcuma (turmeric)
  10. 6 stock cubes
  11. 3 cups of rice (for 6 people)
  12. Orange food colouring
  13. 2 bay leaf, thyme,cinnamon, 6 green cardamom pods
  14. Cooking oil and salt

The Rice

  1. Wash your rice until the water from the rice is clear. (I used 3 cus of rice for 6 people.)
  2. Soak the rice for at least 30 minutes.
  3. To cook the rice , pour water in a large cooking pot. (Put any amount of water as long as it completely covers the rice. There is no need of measuring water since the rice is ony pre cooked in this water,then drained. The rice is baked until fully cooked.) Add green cardamoms, cinnamon and star anise, 1 tablespoon of ghee and salt to taste.
  4. Boil the eggs for 8 minutes only since the egg will comtinue cooking in the sauce then cool them and peel then set aside.
  5. Then transfer your rice on abraking try. Spread evenly
  6. Mix your food colouring with milk, Ionly used 1/2 a tea spoon with 1.2 gass of milk. You can use water if you wish. Sprinkle the food colouring mixture randomly on the rice. At this stage don't mix the rice until it's completely cooked.
  7. Cover the rie with foil. Bake at 170 DC for 35 minutes. After 30 minutes. After 30 minutes, remove the foil and let the rice cook for the remaining 5 minutes.
  8. Remove the rice from the oven and delicately mix with a big spoon to evenly spread around the orange and white rice

The Chicken Biryani

  1. Prepare your chicken (1 used 1.5 kg), remove the chicken skin then ad a teaspoon of ginger paste, garlic paste and 2 to 3 sock cubes.
  2. Add water to the chicken level. Boil the chicken until blood stops oozing from the bones Do not cook the chicken all the way because it wiwill continue cooking in the Biryani sauce.
  3. Chop 4 onions
  4. Heat some oil. When the oil starts smoking, add in your chopped onions.
  5. Heat 6 tablespoons of cooking oil.
  6. Add in your crushed tomatoes. I used three medium tins. You can use 8 to 10 chopped and blend tomatoes.
  7. Stir ad let it cook for 2 to 3 minutes
  8. Add in your spices, teaspoon of garam masala, biryani masala and 1/2 teaspoon of tumeric.
  9. Add in 1 teaspoon of corianer powde, 2 to 3 stock cubes, salt to taste.
  10. Stir and cook for a few minutes covering with a lid because the sauce is ready for the chicken when the oil starts coming up. Please make sure the sauce is well cooked before adding boiled chicken. This is to make sure that th tomato sauce and the spices are well cooked. If the sauce keeps splashing. You will know that the sauce is well cooked before adding boiled chicken. This is to make sure that the tomato sauce and the spices are well cooked. If the sauce and the spices are well coooked .If the sauce starts sticking on the bottom of the cooking pot add some cooking oil and stir.
  11. Stir from time to avoid sticking and burning and let the chicken cook until very soft. Remember to keep the lid on while cooking.
  12. Add the boiled eggs and cook for further 5 minutes.
  13. Add the chopped mint and coriander and put off the heat. Mix well and set aside.
  14. End results of Chicken Biryani
